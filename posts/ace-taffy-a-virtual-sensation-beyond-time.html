Ace Taffy – A Virtual Sensation Beyond Time

Internet figures By Weilin Published: 2026-04-18 00:00
In the ever-expanding universe of virtual YouTubers (VTubers), few personalities shine as brightly or as controversially as Ace Taffy, better known by her Chinese name Yong Chu Taffy. Debuting on June 26, 2021 as an independent personal-streaming VTuber, she has since amassed over 2.2 million followers on China‘s Bilibili platform as of 2026, becoming one of the platform’s most recognizable and polarizing virtual personalities.

Who Is Ace Taffy?

According to her official lore, Ace Taffy is no ordinary streamer — she is a teenage “ace detective” and inventor who owns a detective agency. Born on August 12, 1868 in Wales, she invented a time machine with the intent of leaping one hundred years into the future. However, due to a calibration error, each jump came with a 36-year margin, causing her to arrive in the modern era much later than planned. Once there, she became fascinated with contemporary video games and decided to stay. While her birth certificate says she is over 150 years old, she insists she is forever 17.

Visually, Ace Taffy is characterized by her pink hair, golden eyes, M-shaped bangs, and twin low ponytails — a design crafted by the renowned artist Nanakagura, who is also known as “dog-mom” and previously designed Hololive‘s Chinese talents. Her signature outfit is a detective-style costume complete with goggles, ribbons, and black over-the-knee boots.

What Does She Do?

Ace Taffy’s daily content is a lively mix of gaming streams, singing performances, casual chat sessions, and video reactions. She has released original songs, including Wings of Eternity, a heartfelt composition dedicated to her fans. She is also known for her energetic covers of popular Japanese songs such as the Ninjari Bang Bang cover she released for her first birthday stream.

Beyond streaming, Taffy has made an appearance in the mobile game ”Virtual Ring“ (Xuhuan) as a core character, where her iconic catchphrases and playful mannerisms have been faithfully recreated.

A Unique Style and Fan Culture

One of Taffy’s defining trademarks is her frequent use of the suffix ”Nya” — which she began incorporating after a “magical cat potion” bit during her first birthday stream in August 2021. This “cat girl” persona has become inseparable from her brand, with fans often chanting ”Follow Ace Taffy, nya! Follow Ace Taffy, thank you, nya!” as a communal greeting.

Her fan community is officially named ”Chu Cao Ji“ (literally ”turf chicks“), but inside jokes and affectionate nicknames abound — from ”Ta Nv Yao“ (demoness Ta) to ”Fei Fei Gong Zhu“ (Princess Fei Fei). Taffy is also known for her thick, almost unintelligible Mandarin accent, which has become an endearing running joke among her audience.

From Minor to Major — The Road to Fame

Taffy’s current persona was preceded by two earlier identities: as a Hololive CN talent Hei Tao Ying (Spade Shadow) and as a low-key transitional channel called ”A Bag of Fries, Hehe” . After Hololive withdrew from the Chinese market amid geopolitical tensions, Taffy ”reincarnated“ into her current form — a process known in VTuber slang as a ”tensei.“

Throughout her career, she has weathered countless controversies — from accusations of risqué content and online harassment campaigns to relentless ”leak“ attempts of her personal information. Yet her resilience has become a hallmark of her personality. As fans often joke, if League of Legends player Tarzan had half of Taffy‘s mental fortitude, he’d have survived his own scandals.

Why Does She Matter?

Ace Taffy represents a new wave of Chinese VTubing — one that blends high-concept fiction with unfiltered, sometimes chaotic real-world interaction. She is at once a time-traveling detective, a cat-eared gamer, and an iron-willed entertainer who thrives in an online culture where memes, drama, and genuine connection collide. Whether you love her or hate her, one thing is certain: when Taffy speaks, the internet listens — nya.
