TeamA: The Rising Power of the LOL

LOL By Lin Published: 2026-01-01 00:00
TeamA has established itself as the most significant force in its region since entering the competitive scene in 2020. In 2024, the team won an international championship — the first from its region to do so — defeating a strong European opponent 3-2 in a grand final that went to all five maps. The team also claimed its ninth regional title in May 2026, a record for sustained domestic dominance. On the international stage, TeamA has been a consistent top-three finisher. The current roster features elite talent across all positions, including a multi-time league MVP in the mid lane and a bot laner who has won two international titles. In the 2026 season, TeamA won both regional splits and achieved the rare feat of placing all five starting players on the league's First All-Pro Team. TeamA now enters the international stage pursuing the elusive "Golden Road" — an unprecedented sweep of all major titles in a single season.
