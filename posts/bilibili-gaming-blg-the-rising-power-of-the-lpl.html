Bilibili Gaming (BLG): The Rising Power of the LPL

LOL By Lin Published: 2026-06-01 00:00
Founded on December 18, 2017, Bilibili Gaming (BLG) is a Chinese esports organization established by the video-sharing platform Bilibili. The organization acquired the I May League of Legends roster to form its LPL division and has since grown into one of the most formidable forces in Chinese competitive League of Legends.

BLG’s journey to the top was gradual but relentless. Early achievements included a runner-up finish at the 2018 Demacia Cup and a fourth-place finish in the 2019 LPL Summer Split. The breakthrough came in 2022 when BLG claimed the Demacia Cup championship. The 2023 season marked BLG’s arrival on the international stage, with runner-up finishes in both the LPL Spring Split and MSI, alongside a semifinal appearance at the World Championship. In 2024, BLG cemented their domestic dominance by winning both the LPL Spring and Summer Splits. The crowning achievement arrived in March 2026, when BLG defeated G2 Esports 3-1 in the grand final of the First Stand tournament in São Paulo, securing the organization’s first-ever international League of Legends title.

The current BLG roster is a constellation of elite talent. Top laner Chen “Bin” Zebin, widely regarded as one of the best in his role, is joined by jungler Peng “Xun” Lixun, mid laner Zhuo “knight” Ding—a multiple-time LPL MVP—bot laner Park “Viper” Do-hyeon, a two-time First Stand champion, and support Luo “ON” Wenjun. The team is coached by head coach Yang “Daeny” Dae-in.

In the 2026 LPL Season, BLG has been utterly dominant. They claimed championships in both the first and second splits. During the second split playoffs, BLG suffered an initial upset loss but then staged a miraculous “one-through-five” run—winning five consecutive best-of-five series from the lower bracket to claim the title and qualify for MSI as the LPL’s first seed. In a historic feat, all five BLG players were named to the LPL First All-Pro Team, with knight winning regular-season MVP.

With Bin boldly declaring his intent to defeat LCK rivals at MSI, BLG enters the international stage not just as a title contender, but as a team poised to complete the elusive “Golden Road”—an unprecedented sweep of all major titles in a single season.
