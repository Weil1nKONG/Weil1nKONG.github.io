TeamB: The Rising Power of the LOL

LOL By Lin Published: 2025-01-01 00:00
TeamB made history at an international championship in 2024, becoming the first team from its region to win a global event. The grand final was a dramatic five-map series, with TeamB's star duelist shattering the world record for most kills in a best-of-five series with 111 total kills. He finished atop the leaderboard in three of the five maps and was awarded the tournament MVP trophy. A rookie teammate completed the "royal road" — winning the championship in his debut year after playing in the developmental league just three months prior. Since that victory, TeamB has continued to dominate domestically. By 2025, the core quartet of the roster had been present for all nine of the team's regional titles. TeamB's journey from early international struggles to world champions reflects a steady ascent built on resilience.
